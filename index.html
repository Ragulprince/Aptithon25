Welcome to Aptithon: Where Minds Meet Challenges!

Welcome to Aptithon, a premier intra-college event focused on enhancing the aptitude skills of students across various departments and years. This thrilling competition encompasses facets such as reasoning, verbal and non-verbal abilities, along with challenging puzzles. Designed to foster a competitive spirit, Aptithon provides a platform for participants to showcase their analytical prowess and problem-solving abilities, with a substantial prize amount up for grabs.
